Hello

My name is Matthew Howard (or just Matt). I’m currently a sophomore studying computer engineering with a minor in mathematics at the University of Pennsylvania. In my free time, I serve on the executive board of PennApps, one of the world’s largest hackathons. In particular, I oversee logistics and purchasing. I’m also a member of the Penn Glee Club and The Pennchants a cappella group. This spring, I’m a teaching assistant for CIS 240, "Intro to Computer Architecture".

I spent the last summer in Durham, NC working as an intern for IBM in Cloud and Smarter Infrastructure. While there, I focused on mainframe administration, product testing, and web application development.

Finally, I have a handful of other hobbies I use to fill in the cracks, including web design, photography, arranging music, and hiking.
